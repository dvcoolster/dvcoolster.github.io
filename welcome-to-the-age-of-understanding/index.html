Imagining the Age of Understanding


Welcome to the Age of Understanding

12th November 2017


Hi

Thanks for dropping by. As we begin to talk further with each other, I’ll just lay down all of my thoughts for you. This way whenever we talk, you have a sense of where I am coming from. As I learn from your thoughts, we shall keep the communication going, and hopefully build an exciting future.

Firstly, I don’t work without, Why?

And I like to ask that in all interviews, ‘I don’t know, I am looking to find out.’ is a perfect answer. The problem is when you have not given it a thought. Why do you want to work here, why do you want to work anywhere?

I am lucky that I can afford to solve that problem for myself. Many people aren’t. People have to keep working in their tiring industrial age jobs with no Time or Energy left to think. They are forced to bound their thoughts in a limited Space-Time. To truly maximize the returns of information age, it’s a must that we free human thoughts.

Unless we begin to think deeper with focus, we are heading towards a ‘Noise Age’ rather than ‘Information Age’

In the evolutionary process of our society, technology creates fear. It’s a fact that people are losing their jobs, education system is failing, Governments are closing borders, planet itself is being heavily damaged, people are afraid of terrorism, human DNA has become editable, income divide doesn’t look like stopping.

Where are we heading to? This fear of unknown forces each of us to keep our guard up.

There’s chaos all around, and unless we learn to focus, the information will drift us, unless we develop courage to walk right through the unknown, hold it by its throat and bring it to what we desire, we are not among the best our species has seen. Then again, before all of that we must ask,

What do we desire? What do we fear?

As one begins to broaden the ‘Space-Time’ of thoughts. They are forced to face the truth of ‘Time’ & ‘Death’, and if you keep moving on, like everybody before you, and everybody after you, you will face a simple question, ‘Purpose ?’

I have heard various answers. Many people conclude that there’s no purpose. Many people want to reach ‘God’. Many people want to help humanity. Everyone always wants to be happy, never met a guy who said don’t wanna be happy. In varied forms,
I have seen people find their purpose, themselves in ‘Knowledge’, ‘Love’ or ‘Work’.

You give your life to any of these you’re a genius, all of them, probably _/\_

Searching ‘Why?’ will lead to philosophy, but a philosophy that can’t be practiced is of no use to us. Therefore, we are going to be focussed on actions we do, and results we see.

Personally, when I imagine the year 2100, I think it’s too sexy to leave it afar. I imagine an age of understanding, where we have made it extremely costly for any violence. We’ve all realized that it’s profitable to think, to love, to be honest.

We’ve realized it’s profitable to just be human!

It’s our job to use what separates us from every other animal, what differentiates us from the machines we make, Our Brain.

I know people like to say Data is the fuel of Information age. But, I believe information is the fuel of information age. I discuss this when we ask fundamentally What is any information?

These same words will be understood in as many ways as many people read it. Every individual who’s reading is unique, and processes this data on his own.

Data is everywhere, from the light that enters our eyes, to the sounds we hear, all of the subtleties we observe, spread over time, memories, biases, etc. is processed by our brain. It operates like a mathematical model and produces a resultant information in our mind. A probabilistic quantum interpretation. That information is the real fuel of Information age, on the basis of which we decide all of our actions, not just all the data.

Communication information begins and ends in human brains. I have written pretty deeply about it, our limitation of expression is fundamental, as our thoughts are quantum, our language is binary. Language can never truly express what we think/feel.

If there’s value in our thoughts, then it surely is being wasted. Evidently, a lot is garbage quality, but how do we improve?

Information age fundamentally depends upon we making sure that anyone and everyone can think anywhere, act on it anywhere, and if there’s a possibility of a market out there for his expression, is able to make his living of it. Unless thinking fundamentally helps me improve my living, why should I think?

Meeting other cultures, reading books, traveling, exploring, all of these things help us broaden our thoughts. I have not found a better way to work on thoughts than discussing them fearlessly with the smartest people I know.

Among all of this, at times I used to fear the power of ‘Facebook’, ‘Google’. Will there ever be something that can fundamentally challenge them?

As I write this, I have no doubt that the power of individual is on horizon. In many sense, its already here. But, the friction is high, and as it’s said, “Removing friction is the job of Startup”. There are going to be multiple, multi-Billion Dollar opportunities, it’s inevitable. We shall carry on discussing these in depth and more.

What’s my purpose?

It’s laid all over my thoughts. Fundamentally, though I am a Gamer, and I have never found a better game than ‘Life’. I enjoy being present each moment.

It’s been a long first post. There are directions you can go from here. Feel free to let me know whatever was incomprehensible. Do remember, we are focussing hard, bringing in information from several fields, and attempting to see a faint image of future.

Thinking is hard, communicating is tiresome. But, we must carry on because,

Unless we can dream it, how can we create it?
